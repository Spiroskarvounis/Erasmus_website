About the project

The project "Skills for the Future: Supporting Youth Employability" is an Erasmus+ Training Course aiming to strengthen the capacity of youth workers to guide young people—especially those with fewer opportunities—toward career readiness, digital resilience, and green job pathways.

The objectives are to:

  • Enhance career readiness skills in youth work;
  • Build digital competencies among youth workers;
  • Promote green and sustainable careers;
  • Foster inclusivity and social cohesion;
  • Encourage intercultural understanding and European identity;
  • Strengthen international cooperation and knowledge sharing.
